Aligning Ambition and Alpha

Witherspoon Consulting is a unique resource for firms and businesses looking to grow and increase equity value, for businesses seeking opportunities to market their products and services, and for investors who need thoughtful research and due diligence.

Within-Your-Grasp is a unique resource which specializes in the creation and development of moving image content. We work directly with writers, directors, and producers. In addition, we work with Witherspoon Consulting to provide business strategy and business development advisory and assistance to production companies and filmmakers.

Capabilities

Witherspoon Consulting selectively partners with ambitious firms that have an identifiable edge and that are generating strong uncorrelated returns for investors.

Asset Managers & Operators

Witherspoon Consulting offers high-touch business development strategy consulting to help grow both newer and established firms to the next level. Marketing and communications expertise is at the heart of our advisory, giving firms the ability to focus on their core competency. Witherspoon serves as a true partner in expanding a firm’s business; as such, we dedicate resources and time to the process of thoroughly understanding our clients and their goals.

Investors

With decades of experience in the industry gleaned from both sides of the allocation table, the Witherspoon Consulting team works to identify and evaluate promising, high-quality managers and operators. We source compelling strategies and firms that we believe offer high value-add to investors.

Film Production

Moving image content creation, and specifically feature film and tv production, offer substantial opportunities given recent changes in the entertainment industry. Outlets for creative talent and content distribution are more varied than ever. Witherspoon Consulting and Within-Your-Grasp seek to benefit from these changes, for our clients and for our own proprietary content.
